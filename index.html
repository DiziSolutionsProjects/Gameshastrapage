Welcome To Game Shastra

Gameshastra stands as India’s largest and most enduring independent video game developer, blending passion, innovation, and expertise since 2008. For nearly two decades, we’ve been at the forefront of gaming, supporting some of the industry’s biggest titles while crafting our own unique experiences.

With a rich legacy of collaboration and creation, Gameshastra continues to shape the future of gaming—one pixel, one frame, and one story at a time. Discover the heart of gaming, made in India, for the world.

Our Services

Art Production

Promotional assets like key art, banners, and trailers attract players and build your game’s identity. Key art creates strong first impressions, banners increase visibility, and trailers showcase gameplay to generate excitement. Together, they drive installs, enhance branding, and communicate your game’s unique experience to a wide target audience.

Game Development

Stunning 2D assets, including concept art, UI design, sprites, and textures, bring your game’s vision to life with precision and creativity. These detailed visuals enhance gameplay aesthetics, communicate style, and build immersive worlds. High-quality 2D design ensures strong player engagement, recognisable branding, and a polished, professional final product.

3D Art

High-quality 3D models, textures, and assets are expertly crafted and optimized for performance, ensuring your game stands out with immersive, stunning visuals. These assets enhance realism and aesthetics without compromising speed, creating engaging environments and characters that captivate players while supporting smooth gameplay across multiple devices and platforms seamlessly.

Vehicle Art

Realistic or stylized vehicle models, from cars to spaceships, are designed to integrate seamlessly into your game’s world. These detailed models enhance immersion, support gameplay mechanics, and reflect your unique art direction. High-quality vehicles improve visual storytelling, player experience, and the overall professionalism and appeal of your game project.

Game Development
